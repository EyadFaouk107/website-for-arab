Rod El Farag Axis Bridge

The World's Widest Cable-Stayed Bridge

Project Overview

The Rod El Farag Axis Bridge is one of the most significant infrastructure projects in Egypt's modern history. It serves as a vital link connecting East Cairo to West Cairo, significantly reducing travel time and congestion.

Awarded the Guinness World Record for the widest cable-stayed bridge, it features a width of 67.3 meters and spans the Nile River with breathtaking engineering precision.

Key Features

  • • Width: 67.3 meters (World Record)
  • • Length: 540 meters (cable-stayed portion)
  • • 6 lanes in each direction
  • • Pedestrian glass walkways on both sides

Project Details

  • Client: Ministry of Defense
  • Location: Cairo, Egypt
  • Sector: Infrastructure
  • Status: Completed
  • Year: 2019
